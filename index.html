About Us

Welcome to Swami Awadheshanand Public School

Welcome to SAPs Shri Sadashivrao Patil Shikshan Sanstha, Kamptee, a registered society under the societies registration act of India, established in 1985 under the guidance of Hon'ble Shri. Y. K. Bhoyar, is committed towards serving the cause of Education especially in the rural area like Kamptee. Started with a vision for commitment to offer excellence in the form of quality education, the Sanstha is catering the educational needs of the society through various and professional institutes successfully since last 3 decades.
